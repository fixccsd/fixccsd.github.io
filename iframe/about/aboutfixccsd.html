About FIXCCSD:

FIXCCSD is a student-led movement that advocates for higher salaries and strengthened unions for educators, bus drivers, and janitors. FIXCCSD additionally makes efforts to bring attention to the potentially corrupt district leaders, Board of Trustees members, and even the superintendent. FIXCCSD has no ties with CCEA, yet it is supportive and favors the teachers’ union. FIXCCSD is highly recognized for organizing the student walkout, yet it’ll not be our only course of action, as more is being planned and organized. To be engaged with the FIXCCSD online community, follow the @fixccsd account via Instagram for updates, and message us if you are interested in joining or supporting us. Together, we can provide and empower students, teachers, bus drivers, and faculty with the representation and compensation they deserve.
